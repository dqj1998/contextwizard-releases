# ContextWizard v2.1.0 — What's New

## English

**Jump straight to your bookmarks.** One click opens the Bookmark Manager and scrolls right to the spot you need — no more hunting through lists.

**Smarter search for Asian languages.** Chinese, Japanese, and Korean text is now tokenized accurately, so your search results actually match what you're looking for.

**Reminders that won't miss.** Monthly reminders now land on the exact day you set, and your bookmarked conversations are protected from automatic cleanup.

**Refresh bookmarks on the fly.** The context panel now has a refresh button — reload bookmark groups and see updated selections without reopening the panel.

**Bookmarks that always work.** Gemini links load reliably now, Copilot bookmarks scroll to the right spot, and re-injecting context replaces stale content instead of skipping.
