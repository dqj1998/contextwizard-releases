### ✨ Key Features

#### Automatic Capture — Works While You Do

Conversations are saved in real time as you chat. No buttons to press, no workflows to learn. Intelligent debouncing prevents duplicates, even in fast back-and-forth threads.

**Works out of the box with:** ChatGPT (chatgpt.com), Claude (claude.ai), Gemini, Copilot, Perplexity, Grok, DeepSeek, Qwen, and 14+ platforms.

Need to add an internal or lesser-known AI platform? Custom URL patterns let you extend coverage in seconds. Domain filters give you per-platform control — auto-save everything, or exclude specific sites.

#### Full-Text Search — One Query, Every Platform

Search across thousands of conversations from every AI platform at once. Type one query — get results from titles, summaries, and message bodies across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and more, all in a single result set.

- **Sub-millisecond results** powered by WebAssembly — even with years of history
- **Accurate CJK search** — Chinese, Japanese, and Korean text is tokenized properly, so your queries match the right characters, not garbled fragments
- **Preview context** — see the surrounding conversation without opening the thread
- **Smart filters** — narrow by platform, workspace, or recency instantly

#### AI Workspaces & Smart Bookmarks

Stop organizing by platform. Group conversations by project — Marketing, Architecture, Research, whatever matters to you. Drag and drop to reorganize.

- **Context Injection (v2.0+):** Pick past bookmarks and inject them directly into new conversations. Stop re-explaining context you already worked out.
- **Message-level bookmarks:** Mark the exact message that matters inside a long thread — not just the thread itself.
- **Nested bookmark groups** with instant-undo and automatic empty-group cleanup.

#### Smart Reminders — Never Lose a Follow-Up

Set a reminder on any bookmarked message. Choose Once, Daily, Weekdays, Weekly, or Monthly (by date or weekday). System notifications fire reliably even when the browser is minimized. Click the notification to jump straight to the exact message.

### 🔒 Your Data Never Leaves Your Computer

ContextWizard is built local-first. Everything — every conversation, every search index, every bookmark — is stored in IndexedDB on your own machine.

- **Zero telemetry, zero tracking, zero analytics.** We don't collect anything.
- **100% GDPR and privacy compliant** by default — confidential prompts, proprietary code, and business context stay completely offline.
- **Works fully offline.** No internet needed for any core feature.
- **Cloud Sync (optional, v2.0+):** End-to-end encrypted sync across devices via Google SSO only — no third-party data access. Subscription feature.

> Uninstall anytime? All local data is wiped from your device. Cloud data is retained securely for 30 days, then permanently deleted.

### ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**How much does ContextWizard cost — is it really free?**
Yes. Core features including unlimited captures, bookmarks, workspaces, full-text search, and reminders are 100% free with no artificial caps. Cloud Sync across multiple devices requires a paid subscription.

**How to save ChatGPT conversations automatically?**
ContextWizard captures ChatGPT conversations in real time as you chat on chatgpt.com — no buttons to press, no manual export needed. It works the same way for Claude, Gemini, Copilot, Perplexity, and 14+ other platforms.

**Can I use ContextWizard offline?**
Yes. All core features work fully offline since all data is stored locally via IndexedDB on your machine. Internet is only needed for optional Cloud Sync.

**Is ContextWizard safe — does it affect browser performance?**
It's a passive observer using optimized MutationObserver APIs with intelligent debouncing. There's no impact on browsing speed or memory usage during normal use.

**How to search across all AI platforms at once?**
A single search query in ContextWizard simultaneously searches titles, summaries, and message bodies across every captured platform — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and more — and returns results in one unified list.

**Does it work with ChatGPT Teams or Enterprise?**
It works natively with chatgpt.com including Teams. Enterprise deployments on custom domains are supported via custom URL patterns.

**What happens to my data if I uninstall?**
All locally stored IndexedDB data is immediately wiped from your device. Cloud Sync data (if enabled) is retained securely for 30 days before permanent automatic deletion.

**Is there a way to try it before committing?**
It's free. Install takes 30 seconds. If it doesn't fit your workflow, uninstall and all data is cleaned up automatically — zero commitment.
